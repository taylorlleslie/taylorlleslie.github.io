Introduction

Ragged Surf Shop

Ragged surf shop is a family-owned and operated surf shop that creates quality clothing and surfboards that are ethically made with the intent to last forever. Ragged Surf Shop promotes a positive surf culture lifestyle that builds community through events, short films, blogs, and art exhibitions.

Target audience

Our target markets are young adults and adults aged 20-60, who are interested in surfing, the outdoors, and sustainable brands.

The primary goal of our target market is for them to feel like they are part of a community that enjoys surfing, the arts, and supporting a sustainable brand. Every time a customer leaves our store or an event we host, we want them to feel comforted by their purchases and experience.
